ESS Survey Data - variables - continuous or categorical?

Country - categorical
IDNO - continuous (ordinal)
Year - continuous (ratio)
Total Hrs of TV Watched (tvtot) - continuous (ratio)
Trust in People (ppltrst) - continuous (interval - assuming there is no zero on
the scale)
Sense of Fairness (pplfair) - continuous (interval)
Happiness (happy) - continuous (interval)
How often you meet socially (sclmeet) - continuous (ratio)
How often you take part in social activities (sclact)
Gender     (gndr) - categorical
Age        (agea) - continuous (ratio)
In a Relationship (partner) - categorical
